IT EQUIPMENT SECURITY AND SAFETY GUIDELINES

General Guidelines:
  1. Place IT Equipment on top of a stable surface, within a suitable working area, not near any liquids or where the IT equipment will get wet or damaged accidentally (ex. pantry, kitchen, bathroom, garage).
  2. When IT equipment gets wet, immediately switch-off and have it checked by SFA Support Group.  You may call your SFA Support Hotline for assistance.
  3. IT equipment must be kept out of reach of children at all times.
  4. When IT equipment gets lost, damaged or stolen, immediately report this to your line manager and SFA Support Hotline and file Lost and Damage Report Form.
Portable IT Equipment (laptops, tables, projectors, external hard disks) Guidlines
Use of Portable IT Equipment:
  1. In the office, secure the tablet at all times.
  2. In conference or meeting rooms, always ensure that the rooms are locked when temporarily leaving the area (lunch break, etc.).
  3. At home, be cautious of working near windows and doors where it can easily be stolen.
  4. At cafés and restaurants, ensure a safe distance from beverages and food and do not leave the portable IT equipment unattended.
Storage of Portable IT Equipment
  1. In the office or at home, portable IT equipment should be placed under lock and key (drawer and room, if applicable) when leaving it for an extended period of time (leaving for the night/day or for the weekend).
  2. When staying in a hotel, place the portable IT equipment in a room safe or if it is not possible, in your closed suitcase.
Travelling with Portable IT Equipment:
  1. Do not leave your portable IT equipment unattended in public places such as airports, piers, train/bus stations and hotels. Do not check-in your portable IT equipment, keep it with you.
  2. Do not leave any portable IT equipment inside your vehicle unattended. 

SOFTWARE/EXECUTABLE FILES:

  • - Use only authorized software that has been properly licensed to the Company.
  • - Never install personal software (i.e. non - business software) unless it is authorized.
  • - Always adhere to the Company software Allocation and Control process.
