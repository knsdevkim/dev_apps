Number 1 Supplier Inc.

NUMBER 1 SUPPLIER INC.

Number 1 Supplier, or N1S is the exclusive distributor-partner of Nestle in Negros and Siquijor.
Formerly known as Island Merchants Corporation, N1S started its operations 20 years ago.
As a Nestle distributor, the company consistently provides visibility, availability, logistics, understanding, and excellence.

About Us

N1S’s offices and warehouses are situated in Bacolod, Dumaguete, and Siquijor. Its area coverage spans 1,219 barangays, 38 municipalities, 19 cities, with a population of around 4.3 million. To date, N1S has more than 350 employees, serving over 20,000 customers and counting. Our goal is to ensure N1S remains to be the FMCG distributor of choice in Negros, Dumaguete, and Siquijor.

OUR MISSION

To ensure continuous availability and visibility of Nestle Products in the most sustainable and profitable manner, beneficial to No. 1 Supplier Inc., Nestle Philippines Inc. and trade partners. To ensure continuous development of our people with the right mindset and competencies that follow the effective management standards and discipline to achieve sustainable desired results.

OUR VISION

To be the most preferred consumer goods distributor in the islands of Negros and Siquijor.

Manuel T. Parroco


Our President and CEO, Manuel T. Parroco leads our executive committee. Prior to N1S, Manuel T. Parroco was also the President and CEO of Island Merchants Corporation. Previous to his work here at N1S and IMC, Manuel was the Senior Vice President of the Food Services Division of Nestle Philippines Inc. Manuel earned his MBA from the University of Negros Occidental and earned his Bachelor’s Degree from the University of La Salle. He also completed a program for Executive Development at IMD Switzerland. With years of experience, Manuel T. Parroco leads Number 1 Supplier to success.
